# Product Vision: Water Treatment Lead Gen Site

## Purpose
Generate high-quality leads from residential homeowners seeking water filtration or softening solutions.

## Core Offer
Free water test or quote for:
- Whole house filters
- Water softeners
- Reverse osmosis systems

## Success Criteria

- **Dominant CTA**: "Get a Free Water Test" (single, consistent CTA everywhere)
- **Trust built quickly**: Badges, testimonials, guarantees
- **Easy-to-complete lead form**: Single-column, clear step labeling
- **Clean mobile experience**: Mobile-first, token-driven theming
- **High contrast accessibility**: Conversion-optimized design

## MVP Scope
- Homepage
- Lead funnel (CTA > form > thank you)
- Trust-building sections (testimonials, badges)
- SEO-ready structure (clean, crawlable)
